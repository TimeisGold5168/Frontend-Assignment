FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How to refund?
➕

♦ If you received a damaged or an incorrect item, or you are dissatisfied with your order for any reason, please contact us at mail@letsbook.com
♦ We are committed to resolving any issues with your order in a friendly and efficient manner. If you wish to request a refund for a book, kindly get in touch with us within 3 business days from the date of receiving your order. When contacting us, please provide details about the nature of your dissatisfaction, and if possible, include images.
♦ You have a period of 7 business days to return the item from the date it was delivered to you.
♦ To be eligible for a refund, the item must be returned in unused and pristine condition, along with proof of purchase.
♦ Once received, we will inspect the item to assess the nature of your dissatisfaction. If approved, your item will be refunded within 7 working days.
♦ You will pay the cost of shipping your returned item to us.

Refunds Will Cover:
~Full rental and deposit fees, as well as shipping costs and any additional return shipping fees (if applicable), for items sent to you in error.
~Full rental and deposit fees, as well as shipping costs and any additional return shipping fees (if applicable), for books that are not legible due to issues such as torn or missing pages or faded print.

How long can i rent a book?
➕
The rental period varies depending on the book and can typically range from a few days to several months. You can find the specific rental period listed on the book's page.
How do i return a rented book?
➕
Returning a rented book is easy. Use the pre-paid return label provided, package the book securely, and drop it off at your nearest shipping location or schedule a pickup.
